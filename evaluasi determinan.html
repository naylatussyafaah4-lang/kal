Determinan Matriks dengan Ekspansi Baris#

\[ \det(A)=\sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+k}a_{ik}M_{ik} \]

dengan:

\[ M_{ik}=\det(A_{ik}) \]

dan Aik adalah submatriks hasil menghapus baris ke-i dan kolom ke-k.

1)#

\[ A=\begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \]

Pakai ekspansi baris ke-1:

\[ \det(A)=(-1)^{1+1}a_{11}M_{11}+(-1)^{1+2}a_{12}M_{12} \]

Karena:

\[ a_{11}=-7,\quad a_{12}=-5 \]

maka:

\[ \det(A)=(-1)^2(-7)M_{11}+(-1)^3(-5)M_{12} \]

Cari minor#

Minor M11#

Hapus baris 1 kolom 1:

\[ A_{11}=[4] \]
\[ M_{11}=\det(A_{11})=4 \]

Minor M12#

Hapus baris 1 kolom 2:

\[ A_{12}=[1] \]
\[ M_{12}=\det(A_{12})=1 \]

Substitusi#

\[ \det(A)=(-1)^2(-7)(4)+(-1)^3(-5)(1) \]
\[ =1(-28)+(-1)(-5) \]
\[ =-28+5=-23 \]

Hasil nomor 1#

\[ \boxed{\det(A)=-23} \]

2)#

\[ A=\begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\ 1 & -2 & -1\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Pakai ekspansi baris ke-3 karena ada dua nol.

\[ \det(A)=\sum_{k=1}^{3}(-1)^{3+k}a_{3k}M_{3k} \]
\[ \det(A)=(-1)^{3+1}a_{31}M_{31}+(-1)^{3+2}a_{32}M_{32}+(-1)^{3+3}a_{33}M_{33} \]

Karena:

\[ a_{31}=0,\quad a_{32}=0,\quad a_{33}=1 \]

maka:

\[ \det(A)=(-1)^4(0)M_{31}+(-1)^5(0)M_{32}+(-1)^6(1)M_{33} \]
\[ \det(A)=0+0+M_{33} \]

Cari minor M33#

\[\]

Hapus baris 3 kolom 3:

\[ A_{33}= \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2\ 1 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned} M_{33}=\det(A_{33}) ===================\\\begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} \end{aligned}\end{align} \]
\[ =(0)(-2)-(2)(1) \]
\[ =0-2=-2 \]

Jadi:

\[ \det(A)=M_{33}=-2 \]

Hasil nomor 2#

\[ \boxed{\det(A)=-2} \]

3)#

\[ A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]

Pakai ekspansi baris ke-1:

\[ \det(A)=\sum_{k=1}^{4}(-1)^{1+k}a_{1k}M_{1k} \]

Baris pertama adalah:

\[ [1,,-3,,1,,1] \]

maka:

\[ \det(A)=(-1)^{1+1}(1)M_{11}+(-1)^{1+2}(-3)M_{12}+(-1)^{1+3}(1)M_{13}+(-1)^{1+4}(1)M_{14} \]
\[ \det(A)=M_{11}+3M_{12}+M_{13}-M_{14} \]

Cari M11#

Hapus baris 1 kolom 1:

\[ A_{11}= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ M_{11}= \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ M_{11}= 1\begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \]

Hitung:

\[ \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} =9-1=8 \]
\[ \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} =-3-1=-4 \]
\[ \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =1-(-3)=4 \]

Maka:

\[ M_{11}=1(8)-1(-4)+1(4)=8+4+4=16 \]

Cari M12#

Hapus baris 1 kolom 2:

\[ A_{12}= \begin{bmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ M_{12}= \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ M_{12}= (-3)\begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \]
\[ M_{12}=(-3)(8)-1(-4)+1(4) \]
\[ =-24+4+4=-16 \]

Cari M13#

Hapus baris 1 kolom 3:

\[ A_{13}= \begin{bmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ M_{13}= \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} \]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ M_{13}= (-3)\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} -1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix} +1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \]
\[ \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & -3 \end{vmatrix}=-4 \]
\[ \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}=0 \]

Maka:

\[ M_{13}=(-3)(-4)-1(-4)+1(0) \]
\[ =12+4+0=16 \]

Cari M14#

Hapus baris 1 kolom 4:

\[ A_{14}= \begin{bmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ M_{14}= \begin{vmatrix} -3 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \]

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\[ M_{14}= (-3)\begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} -1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} +1\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix} \]
\[ \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -3\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}=1-(-3)=4 \]
\[ \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 \end{vmatrix}=0 \]

Maka:

\[ M_{14}=(-3)(4)-1(4)+1(0) \]
\[ =-12-4=-16 \]

Substitusi ke rumus utama#

\[ \det(A)=M_{11}+3M_{12}+M_{13}-M_{14} \]
\[ =16+3(-16)+16-(-16) \]
\[ =16-48+16+16 \]
\[ =0 \]

Hasil nomor 3#

\[ \boxed{\det(A)=0} \]

Jawaban Akhir#

  1. \[\boxed{-23}\]
  2. \[\boxed{-2}\]
  3. \[\boxed{0}\]

Invers Matriks dengan Rumus Adjoin#

Diketahui:

\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M*{ji} \]
\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det A}\operatorname{adj}A \]

dengan (M_{ji}) adalah minor dari elemen pada baris (j), kolom (i).

4.#

\[ A=\begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix} \]

Determinan#

\[ \det A=(-7)(4)-(-5)(1) \]
\[ =-28+5=-23 \]

Minor#

\[ M_{11}=\det([4])=4 \]
\[ M_{12}=\det([1])=1 \]
\[ M_{21}=\det([-5])=-5 \]
\[ M_{22}=\det([-7])=-7 \]

Adjoin#

\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{11}=(-1)^{1+1}M*{11}=4 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{12}=(-1)^{1+2}M*{21}=-(-5)=5 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{21}=(-1)^{2+1}M*{12}=-(1)=-1 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{22}=(-1)^{2+2}M*{22}=-7 \]

Maka:

\[ \operatorname{adj}A= \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \]

Invers#

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23}\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix} \]

Hasil nomor 4#

\[ \boxed{ A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} -\frac{4}{23} & -\frac{5}{23}\ \frac{1}{23} & \frac{7}{23} \end{bmatrix}} \]

5.#

\[ A= \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3\ 1 & -2 & -1\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Determinan#

Ekspansi baris ke-3:

\[ \det A= (-1)^{3+1}(0)M_{31} + (-1)^{3+2}(0)M_{32} + (-1)^{3+3}(1)M_{33} \]
\[ \det A=M_{33} \]
\[ M_{33}= \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} =(0)(-2)-(2)(1)=-2 \]

Jadi:

\[ \det A=-2 \]

Minor#

\[ M_{11}= \begin{vmatrix} -2 & -1\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =-2 \]
\[ M_{12}= \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -1\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =1 \]
\[ M_{13}= \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -2\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} =0 \]
\[ M_{21}= \begin{vmatrix} 2 & -3\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =2 \]
\[ M_{22}= \begin{vmatrix} 0 & -3\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} =0 \]
\[ M_{23}= \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} =0 \]
\[ M_{31}= \begin{vmatrix} 2 & -3\ -2 & -1 \end{vmatrix} =(2)(-1)-(-3)(-2)=-2-6=-8 \]
\[ M_{32}= \begin{vmatrix} 0 & -3\ 1 & -1 \end{vmatrix} =(0)(-1)-(-3)(1)=3 \]
\[ M_{33}= \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} =-2 \]

Adjoin#

\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{11}=(-1)^{1+1}M*{11}=-2 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{12}=(-1)^{1+2}M*{21}=-(2)=-2 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{13}=(-1)^{1+3}M*{31}=-8 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{21}=(-1)^{2+1}M*{12}=-(1)=-1 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{22}=(-1)^{2+2}M*{22}=0 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{23}=(-1)^{2+3}M*{32}=-(3)=-3 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{31}=(-1)^{3+1}M*{13}=0 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{32}=(-1)^{3+2}M*{23}=0 \]
\[ (\operatorname{adj}A)*{33}=(-1)^{3+3}M*{33}=-2 \]

Maka:

\[ \operatorname{adj}A= \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8\ -1 & 0 & -3\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \]

Invers#

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{-2} \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8\ -1 & 0 & -3\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \]
\[ A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4\ \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2}\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Hasil nomor 5#

\[ \boxed{ A^{-1}= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4\ \frac{1}{2} & 0 & \frac{3}{2}\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}} \]

6.#

\[ A= \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix} \]

Cek determinan#

Jumlah setiap baris:

\[ 1-3+1+1=0 \]
\[ -3+1+1+1=0 \]
\[ 1+1-3+1=0 \]
\[ 1+1+1-3=0 \]

Maka:

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned} A \begin{bmatrix} 1\ 1\ 1\ 1 \end{bmatrix} =============\\\begin{bmatrix} 0\ 0\ 0\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \end{aligned}\end{align} \]

Karena ada vektor tak nol yang menghasilkan vektor nol, maka matriks singular.

\[ \det A=0 \]

Kesimpulan#

\[ A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det A}\operatorname{adj}A \]

Karena (\det A=0), maka invers tidak ada.

Hasil nomor 6#

\[ \boxed{A^{-1}\text{ tidak ada}} \]

Nomor 6#
